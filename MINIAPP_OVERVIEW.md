## 2. Key Features & Use Cases

### Feature Highlights

- **Leveraged Trading Simulation:** Open LONG and SHORT positions on real historical second-by-second crypto price data using 20x leverage — all without real financial risk.
- **4-Minute Trading Sessions:** Each game session lasts approximately 4 minutes. Players can exit early to take profit, accept a loss, or get liquidated if their free balance runs out.
- **Free & Paid Games:** Every user gets 3 free games per day. Additional games cost approximately $0.01 (1 cent) paid on-chain. Paid games reward players with TPOINT (Tradcast Point) tokens minted directly to their wallet.
- **Energy System & Refills:** Each game consumes one energy unit. Energy regenerates automatically over time, and users can purchase energy packs for additional sessions.
- **TPOINT Token Rewards:** TPOINT is a utility token on the Celo blockchain earned through gameplay. Tokens are currently used for giveaway eligibility and leaderboard prize distribution, with future utility planned.
- **Competitive Leaderboards:** Daily, weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards rank players by profit. Top scorers receive periodic prizes.
- **Daily Streak System:** Users who open the app daily build consecutive streaks, encouraging daily engagement and rewarding consistency.
- **Referral Program:** Each user receives a unique Tradcast invitation key. Successful referrals earn the inviter bonus TPOINT tokens at increasing reward rates.

### Target Market / User Persona

**Primary audience:**
- Crypto beginners and aspiring traders looking to learn leverage trading in a safe, risk-free environment
- Young, gamification-driven users who enjoy competitive, skill-based mobile games

**Secondary audience:**
- Experienced crypto traders and speculators seeking a quick, competitive trading challenge
- Competitive leaderboard players and high-score chasers
- High-risk entertainment seekers drawn to the thrill of simulated leveraged trading

## 7. Compliance & Legal Considerations

### Regulatory Requirements

Tradcast currently operates as a trading simulation game with no real money at risk during gameplay. Since no actual financial trading or wagering takes place, the platform does not fall under financial services regulation at this time.

**Future considerations:** If a TPOINT token launch (e.g., DEX listing, stablecoin swap functionality) or real-money trading features (Treal) are introduced, regulatory review will be conducted to ensure compliance with applicable securities and financial services laws.

The game session fee (~$0.01) constitutes a fee for service (game access), not a financial instrument or wager.

**Disclaimers:** Tradcast's Terms of Service include comprehensive disclaimers stating that TPOINT tokens are not investment instruments, hold no guaranteed monetary value, and should not be acquired with any expectation of financial return. Full Terms of Service and Privacy Policy are published at tradcast.xyz.

### KYC / AML

No KYC or AML verification is currently required. Tradcast does not require account registration — access is granted solely through wallet connection. No personally identifiable information is collected beyond the user's public blockchain wallet address.
